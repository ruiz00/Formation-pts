Pancake Recipe

Description

A simple recipe to make high protein breakfast using onlt 3 ingredients


Prep time: 5 mins
Cooktime: 10 mins
Total time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup self-rising flour
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Combine eggs, cottage cheese, and flour in a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Heat a nonstick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Coat with nonstick spray. Drop 3-tablespoon portions of batter onto the skillet. Cook until edges are set, 1 to 2 minutes. Carefully turn and cook until bottoms are browned, 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Serve with desired toppings, e.g. maple syrup, jam, fresh berries, or powdered sugar.
